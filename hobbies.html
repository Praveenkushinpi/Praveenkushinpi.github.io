🌟 My Hobbies

Outside of coding and building startups, here’s what I enjoy doing in my free time:

🎮 Gaming

I love exploring immersive game worlds and learning from great game design — it inspires my own game development journey.

🎨 Digital Art

Creating art lets me unwind and express stories visually. I post some of my art in the Art Section.

📚 Reading Manga

Manga inspires my creativity with deep storytelling and unique characters. It also boosts my visual narrative skills for games.

🧠 Learning New Tech

I enjoy diving into new technologies like AI, peer-to-peer networking, and creative tools — always staying curious!

🛠️ Blender 3D

Blender’s been one of my favorite creative outlets lately. I just enjoy getting lost in 3D modeling—whether I’m making characters, cool little scenes, or experimenting with lighting and animation. It’s kind of like a digital playground where I get to bring whatever's in my head to life. Sometimes it’s just for fun, other times I challenge myself to try something new. Either way, it's super satisfying to see something start from a blank space and slowly turn into something cool.
